Storyline

A monkey, locked in a zoo, dreamed about the past memories of him with his animal friends. After he woke up, he knew he had to escape from this misery. But escaping isn't easy. He had to face a new, challenging and fun obstacles and challenges.

Background

This game is a 2D platformer game that has Matter.js (A JavaScript realistic 2D physics engine) with challenges, power-ups and more! This game took about 2 months only to be developed with the 2 members of Hatenig Games.
